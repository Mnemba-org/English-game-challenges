📘 Quick Class: Sentence Completion

In this challenge, you'll be asked to choose the correct word to complete a sentence. This helps with both vocabulary and grammar understanding.

Examples:
  • She goes to school every morning. ✅ (Correct tense)
  • They are playing football now. ✅ (Present continuous)
  • He was tired after work. ✅ (Past tense)
  • The cat is under the table. ✅ (Preposition use)
  • I have eaten lunch already. ✅ (Present perfect)

Tips:

  • Pay attention to tenses: past, present, future.
  • Use clues in the sentence like "yesterday", "now", "already" to decide the correct word.
  • Think about subject-verb agreement (e.g., "He runs", not "He run").
