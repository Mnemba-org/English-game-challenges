🧠 Spelling Quick Class

Spelling matters! Misspelling a word can change its meaning or make writing unclear. Let's look at common mistakes:

  • Receive ✅ (Not Recieve) - Remember: "i before e except after c"
  • Definitely ✅ (Not Definately) - No “a” in it!
  • Separate ✅ (Not Seperate) - It has “a” in the middle
  • Accommodation ✅ - Double “c” and double “m”
  • Occasionally ✅ - Two “c”s and one “s”
  • Embarrass ✅ - Two “r”s and two “s”s
  • Definitely vs. Defiantly - Totally different meanings!
  • Effect vs. Affect - Noun vs. Verb
