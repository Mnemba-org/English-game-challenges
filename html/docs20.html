🎧 Quick Class: Narrative Listening Game

Welcome to the Narrative Listening Game! In this activity, you will:

  • 🎙️ Listen to 10 short audio narratives about everyday topics like food, travel, school, or health.
  • ❓ Answer the question: “What was the narrative about?” with four answer choices.
  • 🔁 You can replay each audio if needed to understand better.
  • 🏆 You’ll receive a score out of 10 based on your answers.

🌟 Why is this important for language learners?

  • Improves Listening Skills: Real understanding comes from listening. These short stories help you practice that skill in a fun way.
  • Boosts Vocabulary: You'll hear useful, everyday words used by native speakers.
  • Teaches Natural Expressions: You’ll learn how people actually speak in real conversations.
  • Builds Confidence: The more you understand spoken language, the more confident you’ll feel in real life situations.
  • Trains Your Brain: It helps your brain get used to the rhythm, tone, and flow of the language.
✅ Tip: Don’t worry if you don’t catch everything the first time. Listen again, focus on key words, and take your time!

Click Play Game when you’re ready. Enjoy the game and grow your skills!
