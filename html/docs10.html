In this challenge, you'll find sentences with grammar mistakes. Your task is to identify and correct the mistake to improve your grammar skills.

Examples of common grammar mistakes:
  • Incorrect: She go to school every day.
    Correct: She goes to school every day.
  • Incorrect: They is playing football.
    Correct: They are playing football.
  • Incorrect: I have ate lunch.
    Correct: I have eaten lunch.
  • Incorrect: He don't like apples.
    Correct: He doesn't like apples.

Tips:

  • Look for subject-verb agreement.
  • Check verb tenses carefully.
  • Watch out for commonly confused words.
