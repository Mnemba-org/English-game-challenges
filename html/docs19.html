📝 Quick Class: Daily Morning Routine Phrases

  • Wake up: "I wake up at 6 AM."
  • Brush teeth: "I brush my teeth every morning."
  • Take a shower: Not 'make' or 'do' — correct is "take a shower".
  • Have breakfast: "I usually have tea and bread."
  • Correct use: "I take a shower and then drink tea."
  • Wrong examples: ❌ "I make a shower", ❌ "I eat tea"
