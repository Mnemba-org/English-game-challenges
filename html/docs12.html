📘 Quick Class: Tense Identification

In this challenge, you will read a sentence and identify which tense is being used. Tenses tell us when an action happens — in the past, present, or future.

Examples:
  • She is watching TV. ➡️ Present Continuous ✅
  • They had eaten lunch before 2 PM. ➡️ Past Perfect ✅
  • I will travel next week. ➡️ Simple Future ✅
  • He goes to school every day. ➡️ Simple Present ✅
  • We were playing football. ➡️ Past Continuous ✅

Tips:

  • Check the verb forms: is/are + -ing often means Present Continuous.
  • Words like yesterday, already, will help you spot the tense.
  • Perfect tenses often use have/has/had + past participle.
